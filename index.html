HOW WE SHOULD PREPARE FOR A NOTARY SIGNING

UNDERSTAND

FIRST OF ALL IT IS ESSENTIAL TO KNOW THE TYPE OF NOTARIZATION NEEDED. THE MOST COMMON TYPES OF NOTARIZATION ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS, JURATS, OATHS AND COPY CERTIFICATION. ONE MUST KNOW WHAT TYPE OF NOTARIZATION THE REQUIRE AND SHOULD TELL THE NOTARY. ( THE NOTARY HIMSELD/HERSELF CANNOT ADVISEE ON THE TYPE OF NOTARIZATION SUITABLE FOR YOU AS HE/SHE A MINISTERIAL OFFICIAL IF IN DOUBT. CONTACT AN ATTORNEY.)

IDENTIFICATION

THE CLIENT WILL HAVE TO PRESENT A VALID ID AT THE TIME OF NOTARIZATION. THE NOTARY WILL ENSURE THAT THERE ARE NO DISCREPANCIES BETWEEN THE NAME ON THE ID AND THE NAME ON THE DOCUMENT. THE NOTARYS RESPONSIBILITY WILL BE TO MAKE SURE THEY MATCH.

WITNESSES

OUR NOTARIZERS WILL ENSURE THAT ALL SIGNERS ARE PRESENT PHUSICALLY FOR THE NOTARIZATION. ALL THE SIGNERS SHOULD BE AWRE AND MUST SHOW WILLINGNESS BEFORE THE NOTARY. IF WITNESSES ARE REQUIRED TO COMPLETE THE DOCUMENT AND YOU ARE NOT ABLE TO ATTAIN WITNESS YOURSELD, PLEASE INFORM THE NOTARY AHEAD OF T HE SO THAT THEY CAN TAKE THE NECESSARY STEPS AND REACH OUT TO OUR MOBILE WITNESSES.

QUALITY CHECK

NEXT, WE WILL ENSURE THE DOCUMENTS THAT NEED TO BE NOTARIZED ARE COMPLETELY FILLED AND THAT THERE ARE NO BLAND FIELDS LEFT BEHIND. IF THE DOCUMENT IS INCOMPLETE, THE NOTARY WILL DO ITS BEST TO CATCH THOSE BLANK SPOTS AND ADDRESS WITH THE SIGNER(S). OUR NOTARIES WILL NOTARIZE THE DOCUMENT ONCE THEY FEEL IT IS APPROPRAITELY FILLED AND COMPLETED.

PAYMENT

LASTLY, OUR NOTARIES COME TO EVERY SIGNING WITH THE MOST COMMON ACCEPTABLE FORMS OF PAYMENT. WE ACCEPT ALL MAJOR CREDIT CARDS AND HAVE ALL FORMS OF MOBILE PAYMENT APPS AVAILABLE (ZELLE, CASHAPP, VENMO, AND PAYPAL).
